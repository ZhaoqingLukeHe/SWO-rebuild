Collisions with glass kill 1 billion birds per year in North America

Safe Wings Ottawa is an initiative of the Ottawa Field-Naturalists' Club to reduce bird mortality from window collisions through research, prevention and rescue.

Our mission is to learn more about the extent of the problem in Ottawa, to raise awareness about the causes of window strikes, and to encourage effective solutions.

Explore our site to find out how you can help save birds, one window at a time.
